The Best Movies According to Subhapreet Patro

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Avengers: Endgame

An epic superhero film where the Avengers unite to defeat Thanos and save the universe.

3 Idiots

A heartwarming comedy-drama about friendship, learning, and following your passion.

A nostalgic tale of friendship, resilience, and life lessons set in a college backdrop.
